FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do tennis lessons cost?

Single sessions are $65 for a 1-hour private lesson. 2-person sessions are $100 ($50 per person). All lessons include the court and equipment — no hidden fees.

Do you provide tennis courts and equipment?

Yes. We provide the tennis court and balls for all lessons. Just show up ready to learn and have fun.

Do you offer lessons in San Francisco?

Yes. We serve San Francisco, Daly City, Pacifica, and surrounding Bay Area communities with private tennis lessons.

Are you certified?

Yes. Our coach is CPR and First Aid certified with 15 years of professional tennis coaching experience.
