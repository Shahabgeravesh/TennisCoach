About Us

Rally Tennis Coaching is led by Shahab, who brings 15 years of tennis experience to every lesson in San Francisco, Daly City, and throughout the Bay Area. Our mission is to make tennis fun, accessible, and rewarding for everyone — whether you're a complete beginner or looking to sharpen your game.

With experience coaching multiple after-school programs, Shahab offers customized lessons tailored to your individual needs. The focus is on building confidence, improving technique, and making tennis an enjoyable experience for all ages and skill levels.
