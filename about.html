About Us

Rally Tennis Coaching is led by experienced tennis coach Shahab, a verified Superprof tennis coach who brings 15 years of tennis experience to every lesson in Daly City, CA. Our mission is to make tennis fun, accessible, and rewarding for everyone. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to improve your game, we provide personalized attention and a supportive environment for all ages and skill levels.

With experience coaching multiple after-school programs and a proven ability to connect with players of all ages and skill levels, we offer customized tennis lessons tailored to your individual needs. Our coaching approach focuses on building confidence, improving technique, and making tennis an enjoyable experience for everyone. As a verified Superprof coach, you can trust in our commitment to professional, high-quality tennis instruction.
